How to Find Late-Night Food Near You

Whether you're finishing a late shift, wrapping up a night out, or just hit with a midnight craving, finding a kitchen that's still open can be surprisingly difficult. Most restaurant listing sites show business hours — when the doors are open — but not when the kitchen stops taking orders. That's the gap Letsnarf fills.

We crawl restaurant websites, social media pages, and structured data sources in real time to find actual food-service hours. 💡 Tips for Late-Night Diners

  • Call ahead — Kitchen hours can change on holidays, game nights, or slow evenings. A quick phone call saves a wasted trip.
  • Check the confidence score — We show how confident we are in each venue's hours. Scores below 30% mean you should verify before heading out.
  • Order delivery — If the kitchen closes soon, consider ordering through DoorDash or UberEats using the quick-links on each result card.
  • Look for "food until" clues — Many bars serve food until a set time even though the bar stays open later. Our tool specifically tracks food-service hours, not bar hours.
  • Weekend hours differ — Friday and Saturday kitchens often stay open an hour or two later. Try searching on different nights to compare.

How Letsnarf Finds Kitchen Hours

1

Multi-Source Discovery

We search Google Places, Facebook, Instagram, OpenStreetMap, and Foursquare simultaneously to build a complete picture of each restaurant's food-service schedule.

2

Smart Conflict Resolution

When sources disagree (and they often do), our proprietary Precedence Engine weighs each source by reliability and freshness to determine the most accurate hours.

3

Confidence Scoring

Every result includes a confidence score so you know how reliable the data is. Scores below 30% trigger a "call ahead" warning — we never pretend bad data is good data.

4

Community Verification

Real diners confirm what the data says. When enough people report that a kitchen is still taking orders — or that it's not — those votes are weighed against business listings to sharpen accuracy. Business owners can also set authoritative hours directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Still Serving Food?

Still Serving Food is a free web app that crawls local restaurant and bar websites in real time to find their kitchen hours, grill hours, and hot food service times — so you know exactly which places near you are still serving food right now.

How do I find out which restaurants are still serving food near me?

Enter your city or neighbourhood (e.g. "Brooklyn, NY" or "Manchester, UK") in the search box and click Search. Still Serving Food instantly shows you which nearby bars and restaurants are currently serving food, along with their kitchen closing times.

Does Still Serving Food show kitchen closing times?

Yes. Still Serving Food shows the exact time the kitchen closes at each venue, so you can see how much time you have to order food. It focuses on food service hours — not general opening hours or bar hours.

How accurate is the food hours information?

Food hours are scraped in real time from each venue's own website. While we strive for accuracy, hours can change and may not reflect special closures or seasonal changes. Results are cached for 10 minutes. Always confirm with the venue directly for the most reliable information.
