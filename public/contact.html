Contact Us

We're a small, independent project and we genuinely love hearing from readers, restaurant owners, farmers, food advocates, and anyone passionate about local food systems. Here's how to reach us.

General Enquiries

📧 Email: hello@letsnarf.com

We aim to respond within 2–3 business days.

Story Tips & Restaurant Suggestions

Know a Madison restaurant with an amazing sustainability story? A local farm that deserves coverage? A food waste initiative that's flying under the radar? Email us at tips@letsnarf.com and we'll look into it.

Search Tool Feedback

If you notice that kitchen hours for a specific venue are wrong, or if the search tool isn't returning results for your area, please let us know at support@letsnarf.com. Include the restaurant name and city so we can investigate.

Partnership & Advertising

We use Google AdSense for display advertising across the site. If you're interested in a direct partnership, sponsored content, or other advertising arrangements, please reach out at hello@letsnarf.com with the subject line "Partnership Enquiry."

Note: We only partner with brands and services that genuinely align with local food, sustainability, or the restaurant and hospitality industry.

Press

Media enquiries, interview requests, or requests for data about the site can be sent to press@letsnarf.com.
