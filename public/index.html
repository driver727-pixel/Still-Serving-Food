Frequently Asked Questions

What is Still Serving Food?

Still Serving Food is a content site covering Madison's local food scene, sustainability, and co-op culture — plus a free web tool that crawls restaurant and bar websites in real time to show kitchen hours and which venues are currently serving food.

How do I find restaurants still serving food near me?

Visit the Search page, enter your city or neighbourhood (e.g. "Madison, WI" or "Brooklyn, NY"), and the tool will show you which nearby venues are currently serving food, along with their kitchen closing times.

What is the Madison Food Loop?

The Madison Food Loop is a community initiative in Madison, Wisconsin dedicated to reducing food waste and building a more connected, resilient local food system. Read our full article on the Madison Food Loop to learn more.

How accurate is the kitchen hours information?

Kitchen hours are scraped in real time from each venue's own website and cached for 10 minutes. While we strive for accuracy, hours can change. Always confirm directly with the venue for the most reliable information, especially around holidays or special events.
