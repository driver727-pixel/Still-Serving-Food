Frequently Asked Questions

What does Still Serving Food do?

Still Serving Food is a free search tool and editorial site built around one practical question: who is still serving food right now? We focus on food-service hours such as kitchen, grill, and hot-food service times rather than general business hours.

How do I find places that are still serving food near me?

Go to the Search page, enter your city or neighbourhood, and review the results for venues that are serving now, closing soon, or opening later. The goal is to help you decide quickly where you can still place a food order.

Where do the kitchen hours come from?

Still Serving Food checks a mix of venue websites, social profiles, and structured business data to build the best available estimate of a kitchen's food-service schedule. That gives you a more useful answer than standard listings that only show when the building is open.

How accurate and up to date are the results?

Results are refreshed when a search runs and cached for 10 minutes to keep the experience fast. We surface confidence information when available, but hours can still change unexpectedly, so it is always wise to call the venue around holidays, events, or last-minute schedule changes.

Why can a restaurant be open but not serving food?

Many bars and restaurants keep the bar, patio, or dining room open after the kitchen stops taking orders. We track the food ordering window, so a venue can be open for drinks or seating while no longer serving food.

Can restaurant owners update their listing?

Yes. Verified business owners can claim their venue and publish authoritative hours or temporary closure updates, which helps customers see the most reliable food-service information available.

Do I have to pay to use Still Serving Food?

The search tool is free to try. Your first search each session is free, and additional searches are supported by a short wait or an optional subscription for unlimited access.
